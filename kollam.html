Thenmala EcoTourism

Thenmala translates to honey hill boasts of India's first planned Ecotourism destination.Located 66 kms to the east of Kollam and is a treasure trove of activities. From boat rides to leisurely walks to even adventure sports like mountain biking and rock climbing, have you covered on all fronts

Palaruvi

Translates to "Stream of milk",it falls from a height of 300 feet and is the 32nd highest waterfalls in India.

Sambranikodi

A piece of heaven in Ashtamudi Lake, Samranikodi is a popular place to visit in kollam adorned with lush mangroves.It is believed that during ancient times, small ships from China anchored along this coast. Local people referred to these ships as 'chambrani' and later the place came to be known as Sambranikodi.

Munroe Thuruthu

The place is named in honour of Resident Colonel John Munro of the former Princely State of Travancore

Jatayu Earth Centre

The world’s largest bird sculpture and within it a rock theme park for adventure enthusiasts is what today pays tribute to the mythical bird from the Indian epic Ramayana – Jatayu.

Malamel

Malamel is noted for its beautiful locales and rocky hillocks that offer a picturesque view of the eastern sector of the district.
